Hello! I'm Anojan Yogenthiran, a CS Master's student specializing in Artificial Intelligence. I am passionate about leveraging AI to drive innovation across various domains. My research primarily focuses on applying advanced AI models to fields like healthcare and computational biology. Currently, as a Research Assistant at the Intelligence Data Science Lab, under the supervision of [Dr. Apurva Narayan](https://a-narayan.github.io/), I am using generative AI to identify optimal aptamers for drug discovery, making valuable contributions to advancements in healthcare.

Previously, as an Associate AI/ML Engineer at HeHealth, I led key projects involving the development of deep learning models for medical diagnostics, where I improved the accuracy of STD detection and expanded medical image datasets using GANs. My work emphasizes both innovation and explainability, integrating explainable AI (XAI) techniques to build trust in AI-powered diagnostics. Additionally, during my time at SenzMate AIoT Intelligence as an Associate ML Engineer, I worked on projects involving anomaly detection, optimizing the efficiency of IoT sensor data analysis, and vehicle damage detection.

I received a Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology in 2021. During my undergraduate studies, I interned at SenzMate AIoT Intelligence, where I focused on anomaly detection in IoT sensor data as an ML Engineer intern.

Outside of work, I enjoy watching anime and TV shows, playing soccer, and hiking. I also enjoy trying out new recipes and exploring a wide range of interests, from movies to various sports.
